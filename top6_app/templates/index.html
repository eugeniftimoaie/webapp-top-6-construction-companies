Analysis of Income and Equity

This section briefly analysis the income and equity situation from the shareholders point of view.

The Net Profit Margin (Net Profit / Revenue) gives information on how well the expenses of the enterprice are controlled long-term and how much profit remains to the shareholders. Net Profit Margins in construction industry are relatively low as the classic construction business is very cost intensive and competition in most areas is high. In 2019 the Top 6 companies exept Vinci had Net Profit Margins between 2 and 4 % which are quite common for large construction companies. Most successful companies as for example Microsoft score Profit Margins of 20 % an more in long-term. For the year 2019 Microsoft had an extraordinary Net Profit Margin of 31 %.

The Equity Ratio (Equity / Total Assests) gives information on the total assets which are financed by the shareholders. A good equity ratio might be 30 % and higher. In construction business lower Equity Ratios are common. In 2019 the Top 6 companies had Equity Ratios between 12 and 31 %. Reasons for these modest Ratios might be low Profit Margins, high cost intensivity of construction enterprises, high competition or laws of taxation.

In this comparison I intended to show the growth respectively the trend for a period of at least 10 years. Due to the financial crisis 2008/2009, where we could observe strong drops in market capitalisation, I choosed to start the period from 2007 as this was the last year before the finanical crisis 2008/2009. Accordingly the period ends in 2019 which is the year before the Covid-19 pandemy crisis where we could observe a strong drop of market capitalisation, too.
